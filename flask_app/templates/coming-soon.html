Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I upload a file?

A: Go to the dashboard, click "Upload File," and select the file you want to upload.

Q: Can I share files with others?

A: Yes, select the files you want to share, and use the "Share Selected Files" button.

Q: What formats are supported for imaging protocols?

A: Supported formats include PDF, DICOM, and JPEG.
