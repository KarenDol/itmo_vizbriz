📋 Understanding Your Funnel Stages

🔵 Basic Quiz (Stage A) - Sleep Risk Assessment

Step 1 - Started: User views the introduction page and begins the assessment
Step 2 - Phase 1: User progresses to personal information phase (counted when they reach step 2)
Step 3 - Phase 2: User answers clinical questions about sleep symptoms
Step 4 - Submitted: User completes the assessment and receives risk results
Step 5 - CTA Actions: Unique sessions that clicked on sleep test, consultation, or advanced assessment (max 1 per session)

🟣 Advanced Quiz (Stage B) - Detailed Sleep Analysis

Step 1 - Started: User accesses the advanced assessment from basic quiz results
Step 2 - Phase 1: User completes introduction and understanding of advanced assessment
Step 3 - Phase 2: User answers detailed sleep pattern and lifestyle questions
Step 4 - Submitted: User completes advanced assessment with comprehensive analysis
Step 5 - CTA Actions: Unique sessions that scheduled sleep test or requested consultation (max 1 per session)
💡 Key Insights: Each step represents a critical decision point where users can drop off. High drop-off rates indicate areas for improvement in user experience, form design, or content clarity.
