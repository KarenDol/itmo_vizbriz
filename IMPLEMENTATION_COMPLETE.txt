🎯 WHAT IT DOES:
----------------
✅ Finds the patient's ACTUAL position (highest completed stage)
✅ Identifies the NEXT step in the workflow
✅ Shows workflow progress percentage
✅ Works even if intermediate stages are missing
❌ Does NOT auto-complete missing stages (as you requested)

💻 HOW TO USE IN CODE:
----------------------
from flask_app.services.manifest_service import ManifestService

# Get current and next stage for a patient
stage_info = ManifestService.get_patient_current_and_next_stage(patient_id)

if stage_info:
    print(f"Current: {stage_info['current_stage_name']}")
    print(f"Next: {stage_info['next_stage_name']}")
    print(f"Progress: {stage_info['workflow_completion_percentage']}%")
