How to Scan & Upload Documents

Step-by-step instructions:

  1. Click "Scan with Camera" to open your device camera.
  2. Position each document page in the camera view and capture it.
  3. Repeat step 2 for all pages you want to include in the document.
  4. When you have finished scanning all pages, press the "Upload Document" button.
  5. All scanned pages will be automatically combined into a single PDF document and uploaded in its entirety.

Note: You can also add existing images from your device using the "Add Images" button, or reset and start over using the "Reset" button.
