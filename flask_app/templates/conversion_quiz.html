You might not notice a problem — but your body might.

Issues like brain fog, headaches, mood changes, snoring, low energy, or even difficulty losing weight might be linked to undetected sleep apnea (OSA). OSA is a common condition where your breathing stops during sleep — lowering oxygen and disturbing rest, often without you knowing.

Why this quiz?
