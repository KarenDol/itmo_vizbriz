Authorization for Use and Disclosure of Protected Health Information

I understand that my health information is protected by federal privacy laws. By signing this authorization, I consent to the use and disclosure of my protected health information for the following purposes:

  • Treatment coordination between healthcare providers
  • Billing and insurance processing
  • Quality improvement and research (with de-identified data)
  • Communication with authorized family members or caregivers
  • Appointment scheduling and reminders

Information to be disclosed may include:

  • Medical history and diagnoses
  • Treatment plans and progress notes
  • Sleep study results and test data
  • Insurance and billing information
  • Contact information for appointment scheduling

I understand that:

  • I have the right to revoke this authorization at any time
  • Revocation must be in writing and will not affect actions already taken
  • Information disclosed may be subject to re-disclosure by the recipient
  • I may refuse to sign this authorization without affecting my treatment
  • This authorization expires one year from the date of signature
